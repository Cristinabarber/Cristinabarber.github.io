About me

I am an ecological modeler interested in tropical forests, demography, and remote sensing. Currently, I am a postdoctoral researcher at Michigan State University with Dr. Richard Kobe. My research focuses on the effectsof climate on tree demography in La Selva, in Costa Rica.

In my free time, I like playing almost any kind of sport, including climbing and football. I also enjoy drawing, which I like to use for my research.
