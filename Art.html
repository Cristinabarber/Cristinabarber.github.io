3D printing of an integral projection model (IPM)

Integral projection models are stage-based population models. This kind of model allows testing for the effect of different variables in the population dynamics. Because it is stage-based, it also indentify what stages are the most crucial for population growth. The output of an IPM looks like the graph of the top corner of this image. We thought that printing that chart in 3D will provide a sharper view of how fast the survival and growth rate change with size.
